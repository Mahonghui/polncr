lncRScan-SVM Server: an online tool for predicting lncRNAs

LncRScan-SVM aims at classifying protein-coding transcripts (PCTs) and lncRNA transcripts (LNCTs) using support vector machine (SVM).
Original paper: lncRScan-SVM: A Tool for Predicting Long Non-Coding RNAs Using Support Vector Machine.

lncRScan-TFBS Server: an online tool for analysing transcription factor binding sites of lncRNAs

Long non-coding RNAs (lncRNAs) differ from protein coding genes as they cannot be translated into available amino acid sequences for protein production even though they go through similar transcription procedure as their counterpart. However, it is unknown exactly how such two kinds of genes are regulated differently during expression. The lncRScan-TFBS toolkit is used to analyze the TFBSs of the lncRNAs from several aspects, including reporting basic statistics of input ChIP-Seq peaks of transcription factors (TFs), associating the peaks with transcription start sites (TSSs), conducting motif finding and enrichment analysis for the lncRNA and protein-coding genes by calling several sophisticated programs such as MEME suite, Weeder and PscanChIP.
