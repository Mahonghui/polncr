HAfTs are novel lncRNA transcripts from aflatoxin exposure


last edit: 2018-01-27

On 26 Jan 2018, B.Alex Merrick et al. published a paper in Nature online about the long non-coding RNAs. In summary, they reported novel, differentially expressed lncRNAs after exposure to the genotoxicant, AFB1, prior to neoplastic lesions. Complete cloning and sequencing of such transcripts could pave the way for a new set of sensitive and early prediction markers for chemical hepatocarcinogens.

This article is cited from http://journals.plos.org/plosone/article?id=10.1371/journal.pone.0190992
