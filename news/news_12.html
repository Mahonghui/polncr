Termination of nuclear lncRNA transcription promotes mitochondrial genome maintenance


last edit: 2018-03-13

On 13 Mar 2018, Dorine Jeanne Mariëtte du Mee Maxim Ivanov Joseph Paul Parker Stephen Buratowski Sebastian Marquardt et al. published a paper in Nature online about the long non-coding RNAs. Their results expand the functional and mechanistic implications of non-coding DNA in eukaryotes by highlighting termination of nuclear lncRNA transcription as mechanism to stabilize an organellar genome.

This article is cited from https://elifesciences.org/articles/31989
