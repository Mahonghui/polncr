A 15-long non-coding RNA signature to improve prognosis prediction of cervical squamous cell carcinoma


last edit: 2018-03-20

On 15 Mar 2018, Xiaogang Mao1, Xiaomin Qin1, Lin Li, Jinting Zhou, Min Zhou, Xianxian Li, Ying Xu, Liyun Yuan, Qiong-Na Liu，Hui Xing et al. published a paper in Nature online about the long non-coding RNAs. Their potential functions of this 15-lncRNA expression signature and individual lncRNAs as prognostic targets of cervical cancer were revealed by this study. Furthermore, these findings may have important implications in the understanding of the potential therapeutic method for the cervical squamous cell carcinoma patients.

This article is cited from http://www.gynecologiconcology-online.net/article/S0090-8258(17)31594-9/abstract
