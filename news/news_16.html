HLPI-Ensemble: Prediction of human lncRNA-protein interactions based on ensemble strategy


last edit: 2018-04-01

On 31 Mar 2018,Huan Hu,Li Zhang et al.published a paper in lncRNABlog about the long non-coding RNAs.Their results show that the false positives (FPs) of HLPI-Ensemble models are much lower than that of the previous models, and other evaluation indicators of HLPI-Ensemble models are also higher than those of the previous models. It is further showed that HLPI-Ensemble models are superior in predicting human lncRNA-protein interaction compared with previous models.

This article is cited from https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/abs/10.1080/15476286.2018.1457935
