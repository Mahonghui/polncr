TPGLDA: Novel prediction of associations between lncRNAs and diseases via lncRNA-disease-gene tripartite graph


last edit: 2018-01-25

On 22 Jan 2018, Liang Ding et al. published a paper in Nature online about the long non-coding RNAs. They proposed a novel prediction of lncRNA-disease associations via lncRNA-disease-gene tripartite graph(TPGLDA), which integrates gene-disease associations with lncRNA-disease associations. Compared to previous studies, TPGLDA can be used to better delineate the heterogeneity of coding-non-coding genes-disease association and can effectively identify potential lncRNA-disease associations. After implementing the leave-one-out cross validation, TPGLDA achieves an AUC value of 93.9% which demonstrates its good predictive performance.

This article is cited from https://www.nature.com/articles/s41598-018-19357-3
